Word avalanches

Incredibly contrived setups for homophonic punchlines

A word avalanche is a highly-contrived sentence, where many syllable sounds are repeated via the use of wordplay and/or puns. They can be pretty straightforward, like this:

Mary Poppins' merry pop-ins.

Imagine drag on Imagine Dragons.

Each word avalanche has a rhyming pattern. Try sounding out the next avalanche:

Ice sea captain I see capped in icy cab tin.
