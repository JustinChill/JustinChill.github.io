2021-9-01

The baby boom generation has many choices when considering where to live as they age — but overwhelmingly, seniors would prefer to stay in their own homes. There are many ways to modify a home to enable it's occupants to live there safely, such as improved task lighting, grab bars, and shower seats. Jukebox Health is on a mission to help seniors evaluate their options for home modifications to enable aging-in-place.

We do this by combining home safety assessments by licensed occupational therapists with affordable, CAPS-certified home renovations personalized to each older adult's situation. We help you to make the right choices for your home and lifestyle, and feel secure that your choices have been professionally installed.

In addition, the CAPS designation helps you make your clients’ homes more “visitable.” Even if the home owners don’t think they need additional task lighting, grab bars, and other home modifications for their own use, their family members and visitors might. CAPS helps you help your clients make the right choices, and it gives you more security in remodeling market niche that’s continuing to grow in popularity.

The customer journey

  1. Schedule an appointment with a CAPS certified occupational therapist to assess your home for ease of use and accessability opportunities.
  2. We provide personalized options for making your home safer and easier to navigate. Choose what's most important to you.
  3. At your convenience, we'll send a trained handyman to handle installation for you.
