AI Trends

Making sense of the rapidly developing generative AI space

Artificial Intelligence is advancing at a breakneck pace. New models, safety frameworks, and capabilities appear every week, and staying informed is crucial to understanding the trajectory of LLMs.

Essential Reading

Keeping up with the shift from narrow AI to AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) can be a difficult task. The following resources offer high-level views on timelines, safety, and the future of superintelligence, no RAG required.

  • AI 2027. Analysis and projections regarding the capabilities and economic impacts of AI models expected by the year 2027.

  • AI eats the world. Former a16z partner Ben Evans breaks down the big macro and strategic trends facing our world. I recreate his presentation here.

  • Cartography of generative AI. What set of extractions, agencies and resources allow us to converse online with a text-generating tool or to obtain images in a matter of seconds? Estampa, a collective of programmers, filmmakers and researchers, charts an answer.

  • Statement on AI Risk. The Center for AI Safety's open statement regarding the risks associated with advanced artificial intelligence.

  • Situational Awareness. A comprehensive series covering the decade ahead, the race to AGI, and the transition to superintelligence.
