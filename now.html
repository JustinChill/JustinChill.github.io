Most websites have a link that says "about". It goes to a page that tells you something about the background of that person or business. For short, people just call it an "about page".

Some websites have a link that says "contact". It goes to a page that tells you how to contact this person or business. For short, we call that a "contact page".

So a website with a link that says "now"goes to a page that tells you what this person is focused on at this point in their life. For short, we call it a "now page".
