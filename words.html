Encomium

Encomium: a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly.

The Economic Times, an Indian newspaper, wrote that in retirement he was seen as “an adorable old uncle”. That is an unusual encomium for a business titan—but an especially affectionate one.

Ratan Tata, a consequential and beloved figure in Indian business. The Economist
