Metafy

Great Gaming via Great Training

Thanks to a global pandemic, the videogame industry has seen a surge in demand, with global gaming sales rising to nearly $180 billion in 2020. Metafy is building a marketplace to match gamers with instructors, allowing average players to learn from expert coaches. By focusing on how to make great players become great teachers, both coaches and students win.

Metafy has grown quickly in just 9 months

Metafy caters to aspiring professionals as well as casual players wanting an advantage and parents looking to keep up with their kids. Metafy has raised a total of $8.7M in funding over 2 rounds: $3.2M in December 2020 and $5.5M in May 2021.
