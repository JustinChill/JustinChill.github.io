Common logical fallacies

Statements that make you stop and think twice about what you just heard or read may contain a logical fallacy. They can sometimes be difficult to recognize, but a logical fallacy is an error in logic that makes an argument invalid. When you hear or read something that seems "off" or doesn't make sense, try reviewing these tips:

  • Identify the problem of the position.
  • Look for the lack of logic that prevents a real exploration of issues.
  • Ask for facts, evidence, and independent confirmation of arguments presented.
