Status of World Nuclear Forces 2021

I like to use this bar chart because it appears that an individual named Jeff is in possession of ~10 nuclear bombs. Thankfully there is no Jeff- Jeff stands for ‘Joint Evaluated Fission and Fusion File’ (JEFF). It is a collaboration to get accurate data on fusion and fission. Data is accurate as of June 2021 and is provided by the Federation of American Scientists.
